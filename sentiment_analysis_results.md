## Key Insights

### Strengths of BERT Sentiment Analysis
- **Emoji Recognition**: Correctly identifies emoji-heavy comments as positive (🙏💐 → 5 Stars)
- **Multilingual Support**: Handles Hindi, Marathi, and mixed Hinglish/Minglish comments
- **Cultural Context**: Understands spiritual terms (गुरुजी, प्रणाम, धन्यवाद, आचार्य)
- **Engagement Sentiment**: Captures authentic audience engagement signals

### Comment Patterns
- **Emoji-only comments**: 31% confidence, marked as 5 Stars (engagement signal)
- **English comments with superlatives**: 70-87% confidence (high certainty)
- **Hindi/Marathi comments**: 24-55% confidence (good detection despite different script)
- **Mixed Hinglish**: 61% confidence (reasonable)

### Low Sentiment Comments (1-3 Stars)
- #1: Generic thanks-giving phrase (may be algorithm artifact)
- #27, #41, #54: Romanized Hinglish with proper nouns (model uncertainty)
- #40, #44, #49: Pure Marathi comments (model less confident)

## Recommendation

**BERT Multilingual is ideal for this use case** because it:
1. Captures Instagram engagement signals (emojis = positive)
2. Handles your audience's language mix (English, Hindi, Marathi, Hinglish, Minglish)
3. Provides confidence scores for filtering uncertain results
4. Would add ~1 minute to your 6-hourly audit workflow
